Bob

Click for system insight.

Introduction

Bob works maintenance for sensor grids in one of the first fully automated smart cities. His job consists of ensuring traffic flow, climate control systems, and public safety drones don’t break down. Most days, he runs diagnostics, patch firmware, and file reports that nobody reads.

Backstory

Bob grew up during a period of rapid automation, when artificial intelligence systems quietly replaced large segments of the workforce across sectors like transportation, logistics, and customer service. His parents were employed in regional supply chain operations and were among the last to be displaced by autonomous delivery networks.

Personality Traits

  • Bob has seen how quickly things can change from companies restructure to policies shift. As a result, he's a bit guarded, and don’t take stability for granted.
  • Generally practical and level-headed, with a tendency toward dry humor.
  • Values routines, quiet places, and people who are straightforward.

Relationships

  • Except for an aging aunt, most relationships are polite but distant. It's difficult to connect when everything’s arranged through screens.
  • Keeps in touch with a younger cousin who works in digital policy advocacy, though they often disagree on how much good the system can actually do.
  • Most other interactions are casual—acquaintances from work, neighbors he nods to but don’t really interact.
