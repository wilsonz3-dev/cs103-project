Misty Fields

When standing outside of the town boundaries, the field appears to be empty, no shops, no houses, no roads. Just the occasional gust of wind and wild animal running through. Misty Fields is just a field, with grass covered in dew at all times of the day. Nothing more I promise. Unless... Try hovering over the field, maybe something will appear!!
