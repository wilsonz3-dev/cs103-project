Willow Rapids

Population

Willow Rapids is a very diverse and accepting village, since many people have magical abilities from the river sap. No one is ever scared of abilities that come up, because they likely have one too. The community helps each other a lot because of this, and is very strong through this bond. There aren’t many people within the village, because people don’t really move in and people typically leave because there aren’t many career opportunities. However, the people who stay make the community what it is and keep the traditions alive.

Willow Tree and River

No one really knows where the Willow Tree came from, and why the sap has these magical properties. However, everyone respects the tree and is grateful for the abilities it grants to everyone. As the sap flows through the river, it allows for extremely fertile land, so harvests are always plentiful and healthy. The communal properties of the river and tree build the foundation for the sense of community many feel within Willow Rapids.

The Sunday Mystic Market

Other than Kassandra as the oracle within the village, there are many others with magical abilities. Every Sunday there is a market in the center of the village where people can showcase their abilities, along with selling many mystical objects. There are potions and enchanted objects at the market, and at the market ends with many performing with their abilities. Come down for a tour of the market soon!
