Mae

Introduction

Mae lives in a seaside town. She is a part of a family of fishermen and women, and specifically works on a boat as a diver. This work requires diving for rare crystals that grow in the specific waters that she lives nearby. These are a hot commodity in this world, as owning them and styling them in jewelry and fashion is a sign of status.

Backstory

Mae has lived in this town her entire life. She began fishing and diving with her sisters and parents at a young age. She was brought up doing this, and continues this work as it is a family business. Her sisters do the same, they each work from their own boats.

Personality Traits

  • Diligent
  • Stubborn
  • Thorough
  • Trustworthy
  • Loyal
  • Hard-working

Relationships

Mae is closest with her three sisters, she is the second born. She spends most of her time with them and her nieces and nephews. She is also very close with her mother, as she taught her how to dive and find the crystals.
